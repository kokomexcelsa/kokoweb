Introduction to the Principles of ChatGPT and its GPT Family.

This session was shared in 2023 Global AI Bootcamp. ChatGPT has become a global sensation, reaching 1 million users faster than any other service in history. But have you ever wondered what GPT is and how ChatGPT differs from its GPT family? In this session, I explained the model architecture behind GPT and use simple code with Azure Machine Learning to help the audiences understand and use GPT.

這場是我在 2023 Global AI Bootcamp 分享的。 ChatGPT 已成為全球矚目的服務，比歷史上任何其他服務更快地達到了 100 萬使用者。但你是否曾想過 GPT 是什麼，以及 ChatGPT 與其 GPT 家族有何不同？在本議程中，我解釋了 GPT 背後的模型架構，並使用簡單的程式碼幫助聽眾理解和使用 GPT。
